	So sánh bằng:
		S + V + as + adj/adv + as + N/pronoun
		S + V + not + so/as + adj/adv + N/pronoun
	So sánh hơn:
		S + V + adj/adv + er + than + N/pronoun
		S + V + more + adj/adv + than + N/pronoun
